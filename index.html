Let Us Guide You

At the end of your life, you don’t have to walk alone. We will sit in vigil with you, advocate for you and the death you choose, and help support you and your family in the ways you choose through this final life transition.

What We Do

A Death Doula is different from Hospice because the focus is on emotional needs and spiritual support, rather than medical needs. We are advocates for the dying, ready to honor their path, hold space for emotions, and provide compassionate companionship and support at the end of life.

We help guide clients to put together the things that are important for them to do/have before they die. Whether it is gathering important documents, putting together an end-of-life care plan, holding space for difficult emotions, doing a life review, doing guided meditations to help ease anxiety and pain, or talking about beliefs and thoughts, we can help guide our clients to a place of peace and closure at the end of their lives.

We provide caregiver respite, as we should all practice self-care, especially when we are caring for others. I am sure you have heard the phrase, “Before assisting others, put on your own oxygen mask first.”
