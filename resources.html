Additional Support

At the end of your life or the life of a loved one, you may want additional support or services outside of what we provide at End of Life Guides Marquette. A healing massage, a beautiful handcrafted urn for your ashes, or an alternative funeral service for when you are gone. While we cannot provide these services ourselves, we can recommend skilled professionals in the Marquette area who specialize in these areas. Below is a list of some of the providers, businesses, and organizations that we regularly work with and recommend to our clients.
