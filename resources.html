Additional Support

At the end of your life or the life of a loved one, you may want additional support or services outside of what we provide at End of Life Guides Marquette: a healing massage, beautiful flowers for your bedside, or a handcrafted urn for your ashes. While we cannot provide these services ourselves, we can recommend skilled professionals in the Marquette area who specialize in these areas. Below is a list of some of the providers, businesses, and organizations that we regularly work with and recommend to our clients.

Big Lake Body Works

Our therapists offer a variety of massage services to ensure your treatment is therapeutic, relaxing, and tailored to your specific needs.

Learn more on their website.

Samara Floral Co (Negaunee)

Buying flowers can be overwhelming and intimidating. But don't worry – we're here to help. We know flowers. Whether it's a small gesture to say "I'm thinking of you" or planning your next extravagant event, we've got you covered. All our arrangements are expertly crafted at our studio in Negaunee. We work with local growers and grow our own flowers to source the best of what's in season. We take great pride in providing phenomenal customer service, expert advice and long-lasting designs.

Learn more on their website.

Flower Works (Marquette)

Flower Works proudly offers unique, luxury, high-quality flowers and plants at fair prices to our Marquette, Michigan community. To visit Flower Works is to experience happiness and joy through plants and flowers.

Whether you are looking to surprise that special someone with a lovingly crafted custom flower arrangement, or you want to take home a plant to love and tend to for years to come, we are here to help. Plants and flowers speak when words simply aren’t enough.

Learn more on their website.

Ann Russ Ceramics

In my studio, I craft ceramic vessels to honor the preciousness of life. Each memorial urn, reliquary, and Spirit bowl is hand-built with care, reflecting life’s sacred transitions. My work is inspired by wonder, gratitude for life’s mysteries, and the quiet beauty of nature.

I believe in offering people something handmade with heart and intention—an alternative to mass-produced pieces that lack human touch.

If you're looking for something truly meaningful, I’d love to create a special piece with you. Feel free to reach out or explore my gallery - many pieces are available, and if not, I’d be happy to craft something similar in spirit just for you...

Learn more on their website.
